About E-Cell NIT Meghalaya

The Entrepreneurship Cell of NIT Meghalaya is dedicated to nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit among students and creating a vibrant startup ecosystem within the institute.

Empowering Student Entrepreneurs

Since our inception, E-Cell NIT Meghalaya has been at the forefront of promoting entrepreneurship and innovation. We provide a platform for students to explore their entrepreneurial potential through various initiatives, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Our comprehensive approach includes mentorship programs, startup incubation, funding guidance, and industry connections that help transform innovative ideas into successful business ventures.

Our Mission

To foster entrepreneurial thinking and innovation among students, providing them with the tools and network needed to transform ideas into successful ventures.

Our Vision

To be the leading entrepreneurship hub in Northeast India, creating a thriving ecosystem of student entrepreneurs and innovators.
